## Miaolin B2B AI Smart Distribution Platform – Contract-Based Summary

This document summarizes the **core scope and data responsibilities** from the signed contract `Miaolin B2B AI Smart Distribution Platform (contract).pdf`, focusing on what Miaolin must provide for Phase 1 daily file-based integration.

---

### 1. Project Scope (From Contract)

- **Platform goal:** Build a **B2B AI Smart Distribution Platform** for Miaolin Foods, acting as a digital hub between:
    - Existing **Digiwin ERP** (system of record for inventory, fulfillment, accounting),
    - Frontend B2B portal / website (headless),
    - Line and other order channels,
    - Craveva AI layer (ordering assistant, sales agent, analytics).
- **Integration model for Phase 1:**
    - **Daily file exchange** (Excel/CSV) between Digiwin and Craveva.
    - No real-time API to Digiwin during Phase 1; Digiwin remains the fulfillment system.
- **Key modules confirmed in contract:**
    - Multi-brand product and inventory hub (master data for products and stock).
    - Virtual SKU Mapper (Unit → Carton mapping for Digiwin).
    - 5-Level B2B Pricing Engine (base/public/tier/client contract/volume).
    - B2B customer portal and account structure (HQ vs franchisees, etc.).
    - AI Procurement Assistant (Line/text ordering).
    - AI Sales Agent (product Q&A).
    - Data middle platform and reporting (within Phase 1 constraints).

---

### 2. Data Responsibilities – What Miaolin Must Provide

The contract confirms that **Miaolin is responsible for providing accurate and timely data** via files. These responsibilities align with, and confirm, the requirement list already captured for Phase 1.

#### 2.1. One-Time / Initial Master Data

- **Customer Master (`Miaolin Customer.xlsx` – FINAL)**
    - Complete, deduplicated list of active B2B customers.
    - Includes codes, names, addresses, contact numbers, tax IDs.
    - Includes business segmentation fields used in current operations (salesperson, channel, type, payment terms, etc.).

- **Product Master (`Miaolin Product.xlsx` – FINAL)**
    - Complete master for all sellable SKUs (unit and box SKUs as used in Digiwin).
    - Includes all attributes needed for pricing, inventory, and AI product search (names, specs, units, brand, grade, source, shelf life, storage, standard price, carton price).

- **Digiwin ERP Standard Import Template (`Digiwin_Standard_Import_Template.xlsx`)**
    - The official template Miaolin uses to import orders into Digiwin.
    - Contractually, Miaolin must:
        - Supply this template to Craveva.
        - Clearly indicate the **mandatory columns** (Client Code, Inventory No./SKU, Warehouse Code, Quantity, Unit, etc.).
    - This is the basis for the **Virtual SKU Mapper** and for Craveva’s nightly export back into Digiwin.

#### 2.2. Recurring Daily Files from Digiwin → Craveva

- **Inventory Export (`Digiwin_Inventory_Export.xlsx`)**
    - Exported each morning, per contract “daily inventory sync” clause.
    - Contains at least: SKU/Inventory No., Warehouse Code, Quantity On Hand.
    - Optional but recommended: Batch number and expiry date for batch-sensitive items.

- **Client Price Export (`Digiwin_Client_Price_Table.xlsx`)**
    - Daily export of pricing for B2B clients, supporting the 5-level pricing engine.
    - Contains per-row: Client Code, Product SKU, Client-specific contract price.
    - May also include currency, effective/expiry dates, or price list type, depending on Digiwin configuration.

- **Order Status Export (`Digiwin_Order_Status_Export.xlsx`)**
    - Periodic (e.g. hourly or end-of-day) export of order statuses from Digiwin.
    - Contains: Order ID, fulfillment status, delivery date/time.
    - Enables Craveva to:
        - Answer “where is my order?” via AI.
        - Reconcile inventory vs confirmed/delivered/cancelled orders.

#### 2.3. Files Generated by Craveva → Import into Digiwin

- **Daily Order Summary (`Craveva_Daily_Order_Summary_for_Digiwin.xlsx`)**
    - Defined in the contract as the **standard outbound file** from Craveva to Digiwin.
    - Format must align with `Digiwin_Standard_Import_Template.xlsx`.
    - Miaolin is responsible for:
        - Importing this file into Digiwin daily.
        - Defining how duplicate/erroneous imports should be handled in Digiwin.

> Note: This file is **generated by Craveva**, not provided by Miaolin, but the contract requires Miaolin to accept and operationalize this file in their Digiwin process.

### 3. AI-Related Deliverables – Data Required from Miaolin

The contract’s AI clauses confirm that Miaolin must provide **training data and knowledge documents** to enable the promised AI capabilities.

- **Line / Text Order Chat Samples (`Line_Order_Chat_Samples.zip`)**
    - 50–100 anonymized real conversations where customers place orders via Line or similar channels.
    - Must include variations such as “same as last time”, “add 2 cartons of butter”, “usual flour”, mixed language messages, etc.
    - Used to train the **AI Procurement Assistant** to match contract scenarios.

- **Product Knowledge Base (folder `Brand_Product_Specs/`)**
    - Product catalogs and technical spec PDFs for all Miaolin agency brands.
    - Enables the **AI Sales Agent** to answer questions about fat content, origin, usage, etc.
    - Contractually necessary so that AI responses can be considered aligned with official brand information.

---

### 4. Operational Obligations Highlighted in the Contract

- **NDA & Data Protection**
    - NDA must be signed (noted as done) before Miaolin shares any ERP data, client lists, or Line logs.
    - Miaolin remains data owner; Craveva operates as processor.

- **File Exchange Schedule**
    - Morning: Inventory and client price exports from Digiwin to Craveva.
    - Trading hours: Orders captured via Line/Web on Craveva.
    - Night / Next morning: Craveva exports daily order summary for Digiwin import.
    - Optional: Order status export schedule (e.g. hourly or daily) for synchronization.

- **Data Quality & Template Finalization**
    - Miaolin is responsible for:
        - Ensuring exported files follow agreed templates.
        - Marking required fields in the Digiwin import template.
        - Participating in data cleansing and SKU mapping workshops.

---

### 5. Final Checklist for Miaolin (Contract-Confirmed)

**Initial / One-time data:**

- `Miaolin Customer.xlsx` – final customer master.
- `Miaolin Product.xlsx` – final product master.
- `Digiwin_Standard_Import_Template.xlsx` – with required fields clearly indicated.

**Daily / recurring files from Digiwin to Craveva:**

- `Digiwin_Inventory_Export.xlsx` – inventory by warehouse and SKU.
- `Digiwin_Client_Price_Table.xlsx` – client-specific pricing by client + SKU.
- `Digiwin_Order_Status_Export.xlsx` – order status updates (frequency agreed during implementation).

**AI training & knowledge:**

- `Line_Order_Chat_Samples.zip` – real Line/text order conversations (anonymized).
- `Brand_Product_Specs/` – folder of product spec PDFs/catalogs.

These items together represent the **contractually expected data inputs from Miaolin** to fully operate Phase 1 of the B2B AI Smart Distribution Platform.
